About MedTarget

MedTarget is a unique healthcare venture aimed at improving the lives of patients across Bangladesh. We provide innovative solutions and services that not only allow patients to get better access to the medicine they need when they need it, but also improve the efficiency of pharmacies and medical practices across the country.

Our team at MedTarget have decades of experience in the healthcare industry across the globe, and work with manufacturers, pharmacies, and healthcare organizations to drive improvements in patient care in a cost-effective way.

Testimonials

Using MedTargetRX, we have been able to better understand and care for patients and improve communication with pharmacies. The system has streamlined our workflows, making pharmacy and patient management more efficient, and allowing us to run our business with a focus on the patients, not processes.

MedTargetRX has revolutionized the way we run our practice, increasing the efficiency of the way we issue prescriptions, and improving the way we deal with patients details. We can now provide a better service to patients, ensuring we have the time to offer them the highest quality of treatment, all while staying compliant and cutting costs.
