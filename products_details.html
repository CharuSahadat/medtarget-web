Pharmacy management

With MedTarget’s pharmacy management solution, pharmacies that are using MedTargetRx can pull up the prescription on their computers or tablets/iPads and fulfill the order using the identification number. All for the cost of 1000 taka per month (the equivalent of less than $10 per month).

The benefits of MedTargetRX’s Pharmacy management system

  • • Analytic capabilities all you to produce powerful reports and more easily spot trends
  • • More efficient processing of prescriptions & issuing of medicine
  • • Auto creation of Purchase Order based on threshold limits
  • • Better inventory management at pharmacies
  • • Manage stock at multiple pharmacies
  • • Streamlined workflows

Patient management

MedTargetRX’s cloud based system allows doctors to look up patient records anywhere and anytime, ensuring that you always issue the correct prescription and avoid any legal action due to the wrong medication being issued to patients. All information is stored in one place and is always accurate and up-to-date.

MedTarget’s patient management solution that allows doctors to issue electronic prescriptions with an unique identification number, enabling patients to obtain the medicine from any pharmacy that uses MedTargetRx, even when they have lost the paper copy. This is crucial due to a court ruling that doctor must write easy-to-read prescriptions in block letters or type them to prevent pharmacies dispensing the wrong medicine.

The benefits of MedTargetRX’s patient management system

  • • Access to all patient records at your fingertips, anywhere, on any device
  • • Better communication between doctors, pharmacies, and patients
  • • Fulfill prescriptions more efficiently and cost effectively
  • • Streamlined workflows for patient treatments
  • • Ability to quickly issue prescriptions
  • • Schedule patient appointments
  • • Improved process transparency
  • • Manage Lab reports
